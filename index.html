Helen M. Rowe

Partner, International Law, Sterling Legal Advisory LLP
Helen M. Rowe is a British lawyer with more than 20 years of experience in international and corporate law.

Professional Background

Throughout her career, Helen has advised clients in complex litigation and arbitration cases in the United Kingdom, the European Union, and offshore jurisdictions including the British Virgin Islands and the Cayman Islands.

Core Expertise

  • Corporate and financial law
  • Resolution of cross-border commercial disputes
  • Advising on company liquidation and restructuring
  • Investigation of fraud, misuse of corporate structures, and money laundering
  • Cooperation with governmental and international regulators

Expertise and Achievements

  • Advising clients on compliance with corporate and financial regulations in international transactions
  • Uncovering and documenting instances of corporate document falsification
  • Protecting the rights of creditors, employees, and investors in cross-border disputes
  • Representing clients before the High Court of Justice in London and international arbitration institutions

International Cooperation

Helen coordinates Sterling Legal Advisory LLP's collaboration with international law firms in the UK, Europe, and the Caribbean. She has led joint projects with Collas Crill L.P. (BVI) and other partners, ensuring effective legal protection in complex cross-border matters.

Education and Professional Memberships

Helen received her legal education in the United Kingdom and is a member of the Law Society of England and Wales. She regularly contributes to professional publications and speaks at international conferences and seminars on corporate law, cross-border disputes, and financial crime prevention.
